# Product Roadmap

**Status:** Directional
**Audience:** Everyone
**Canonical for:** The order of defined product outcomes

The roadmap connects the long-term [product vision](./vision.md) to concrete
milestones. It describes outcomes, not a feature backlog or delivery calendar.

## Now: Make The Assistant Dependable

The current goal is to prove that the assistant behaves reliably across
ordinary questions, research, calculations, multi-step work, and memory. The
result must be repeatable and its evidence, decisions, model usage, latency, and
cost must be inspectable.

See the [current milestone](./current-milestone.md) for its evaluation set and
acceptance criteria.

## Later

Later milestones have not been ordered or committed yet. The product vision
identifies likely areas of expansion:

- comfortable mobile access;
- multimodal interaction;
- richer file and artifact work;
- controlled code and script execution;
- integrations with external applications;
- stronger permissions, isolation, and audit controls.

Define a new milestone only when its outcome and evidence of completion are
clear. Do not turn this page into an unprioritized feature list.
